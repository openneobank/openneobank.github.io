FAQ

Questions worth answering upfront.

Is Open Neobank a licensed bank?

No — Open Neobank is open-source software, not a chartered bank. It gives you the ledger, payments, and compliance plumbing to run on top of a banking license you hold yourself, or through a licensed partner. Think of it as the technology layer, not the legal one.

What license is the code under?

MIT. Use it commercially or non-commercially, modify it, white-label it — no royalties and no usage caps.

Can I customize it for my market?

Yes. Fork the repo, theme the app, and extend the ledger or payment adapters to match the currencies, rails, and rules your market needs.

Do I have to self-host it?

No. Self-host on your own infrastructure for full control, or run the same open-source core on our managed cloud if you'd rather not operate servers.
